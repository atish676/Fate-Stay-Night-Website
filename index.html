What is Fate?


Fate/Stay Night is an Anime series adapted from the Visual Novel of the same name. Developed by Type-Moon, the plot focuses on a young mage named Emiya Shirou who becomes a Master in a death battle between Servants known as the Holy Grail War.

The series has three routes- Fate, Unlimited Blade Works and Heaven's Feel which are to be played in the exact same order. Through each route, Shirou bonds with a different heroine and confronts different mages who participate in the war.

The Master-Servant pair who wins the war gets one of their wishes granted by the Holy Grail.

Genius Behind Everything


Kinoko Nasu, born 28th November, 1973 is a Japanese author best known for his Light Novel- Kara No Kyoukai(Garden of Sinners) and Visual Novels- Tsukihime and Fate/Stay Night.

Founder of Type-Moon along with his friend Takashi Takeuchi, he started working on Tsukihime's Visual Novel. Following it's successes he released Fate/Stay Night and Kara No Kyoukai.

Few of his other notable works include- Kagetsu Tohya(Sequel to Tsukihime), Fate/Hollow Ataraxia(Sequel to Fate/Stay Night) and Video Game- Fate/Grand Order.

Standard Classes


In a Holy War, 7 Servants are summoned to fight against each other. Each Servant has a Class, Active Skills, Passive Skills, and Noble Phantasm. A Servant cannot be repeated by Class thus each servant needs to be from a different Class.

Usually, the Servant summoned is from the Standard Class consisting Saber, Archer, Lancer, Rider, Caster, Assassin and Berserker. Saber, Archer and Lancer further getting divided into Knight Class and Rider, Caster, Assassin and Berserker into Cavalry Class.

Every Class has some Class Skills giving them an edge against other Servants like Saber has A rank Strength and High Magic Resistance thus becoming a potential enemy to Caster with A rank Mana, but weak against Berserker with Mad Enhancement, a Class Skill which enhances every other skill with the price of losing sanity and draining large amounts of Mana from their Master, thus outfighting Saber.

Similarly, Archer and Lancer are potential enemies. Also, Archer's Class Skill, Independent Action allows him to stay alive without any master for few days thus increasing chances of betrayal which can be suppressed using Command Spells.

Extra Classes


Besides the 7 Standard Classes of Servants there also exists Extra Classes of Servants which usually are not used in the Holy Wars but sometimes as "a precaution" or as "an edge to win the war" can be used.

There are a total of 18 Extra Classes- Alter Ego, Ancient Giant, Ancient God, Avenger, Beasts, Faker, Foreigner, Funny Vamp, Gatekeeper, Grand Servants, Gunner, Moon Cancer, Non-classed Servants, Ruler, Saver, Shielder, Voyager and Watcher. Wow, its actually a complete clusterfuck!

The Class Skills of many of the Extra Classes are still unknown and even on which basis a Servant can be categorized in an Extra Class is also sometimes unknown.

An Extra Class Servant, Ruler named Jeanne D'arc can be found in the anime Fate/Apocrypha deployed by the Church to overseer the Holy War as a precautionary measure. Rulers are powerful beings with resistance to magic, the ability to ascertain the natures of their foes and the ability to use Command Spells to administer binding orders, generally a privilege reserved for Masters.

Another Extra Class Servant, Avenger named Angra Mainyu can be found in 3rd, 4th, 5th and 6th Holy Grail War but not spoiling much I advise to finish the main storyline of Fate/Stay Night and then come back for detailed explanation.
